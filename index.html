Welcome

to GOM SHABU SHABU at Johns Creek

Korean hotpot dish of thinly sliced meat and vegetables boiled in water and served with dipping sauces.

PHOTO STREAM

Cook your own hotpot with fresh meat or seafood and vegetables

Cook your choice of meat or seafood and veggies (at your own pace) in a pot of boiling water seasoned to your liking. Dip each bite in Ponzu, our citrus-based soy sauce, or in Goma, which is our sesame and peanut based sauce. Create a taste all your own with a variety of condiments, including pressed garlic, green onions and white radish.
